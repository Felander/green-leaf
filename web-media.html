Web Media

Greenleaf Web Media provides a variety of services to assist business communication across industries.

  • Recording Live Events - Monthly or Quarterly Town Hall Meeting, sales Presentations, Latest medical advances
  • Pre-recording/Playback - Get your presentation done correctly without mispronunciations, embarrassing typos or technical issues - Allows for multiple speakers in different geographical locations and time zones can be captured and played back in one presentation.
  • Hosting your content - from either a live event or a prerecorded event we can host and deliver links for viewing of your content at the convenience of your audience.
  • Recreating your event
  • File Conversion - We take a file format that is not as accessible to your audience and convert it to one that is more widely acceptable.

At Greenleaf, we feel that by focusing on these elements of Web Media, the things we have been doing longer than just about anyone in the industry, we can do it better.
